HISTORY

   

Welcome to River Academy

 

Founded in the year of 1987, our esteemed institution, nestled in the heart of Quezon City, stands as a beacon of knowledge. The visionary behind our inception, Dr. George Tolentino, envisioned more than just an academy; he aspired to create a dynamic hub where the flames of scientific knowledge would be kindled, fostering a spirit of collaboration among STEM professionals.

Since our inception, we have remained unwaveringly committed to the pursuit of excellence. Our mission extends beyond the mere transmission of knowledge; it is a dedication to empowering the next generation. Through a tapestry of innovative education and research initiatives, we strive to equip our students with the tools they need to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of science and technology.

As we traverse the corridors of time, our legacy is defined by the countless individuals who have walked through our doors, each leaving an indelible mark on the world of science. We stand as a testament to the enduring power of education and the transformative impact it can have on both individuals and society.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
