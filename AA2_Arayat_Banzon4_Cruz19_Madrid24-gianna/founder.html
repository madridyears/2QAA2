OUR FOUNDER

   

Meet Dr. George Tolentino, Our Founder

 

In the vibrant tapestry of Philippine education history, the name George Tolentino I stands out as a visionary pioneer in the realm of science education. Born on a serene island in the Visayas archipelago, George Tolentino I grew up with an insatiable curiosity about the natural world. Legend has it that as a child, he spent hours exploring the lush landscapes of his hometown, collecting specimens of flora and fauna, and conducting rudimentary experiments in his makeshift backyard laboratory.

His passion for science took him to the bustling streets of Manila, where he pursued a degree in physics at the prestigious University of the Philippines. Inspired by the academic giants of his time, Tolentino immersed himself in groundbreaking research, earning accolades for his innovative contributions to the field.

Driven by a sense of duty to share this wisdom with the world, Tolentino resolved to establish an institution that would serve as the crucible for nurturing the brightest scientific minds in the country.

In 1985, George Tolentino I founded River Academy on the land that his family owned for generations in Quezon City. With a focus on hands-on experimentation and interdisciplinary learning, River Academy quickly gained a reputation as a haven for budding scientists and inquisitive minds. The academy's curriculum blended traditional teachings with Tolentino's newfound ancient knowledge, creating a unique and holistic approach to science education.

Under Tolentino's leadership, River Academy produced a generation of brilliant scientists who went on to make groundbreaking discoveries in fields ranging from astrophysics to marine biology. The academy's research facilities became renowned for pushing the boundaries of scientific understanding, and its alumni formed an illustrious network that influenced the trajectory of science in the Philippines.

River Academy became synonymous with excellence in science education in the Philippines. The institution continues to thrive, inspiring generations of students to explore the wonders of the natural world and push the boundaries of human knowledge.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
