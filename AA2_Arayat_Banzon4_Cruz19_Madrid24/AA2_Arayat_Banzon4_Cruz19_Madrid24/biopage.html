Biology 1


What does Biology 1 provide the students?

When taking Biology I, every student will have the chance to gain a deeper understanding of the natural world's beauty and life cycles. As they learn about the lives of organisms and Mother Nature, every student will develop a newfound fascination love for both Mother Nature and their own bodies as they are taught about the lifestyles of organisms and how millions upon millions of tiny little cells are somehow the building blocks of life itself!

Through field visits and interactive experiments planned and supervised by the school, students will have the chance to see firsthand how the world operates. Experts in the field of biology frequently visit the school as guest lecturers; some of them are even our own alumni returning the favour by sharing their knowledge and experiences from their youth. Since we provide our students the freedom to pursue their genuine curiosity and make real use of their technical skills, they laud our programme as being among the best in the nation for being able to build a strong foundation for the students for the later years of their education.

How does Biology 1 test the knowledge of their students?

Biology 1 makes use of standardized tests, practical assessments, and summative examinations to test the knowledge of their students and to ensure that they have acquired the necessary fundamental skills and knowledge they need. However, we do take into factor the physical and mental health of our students to create an educational environment that is both sympathetic and fair to the student so they may grow into a well developed and stable member of society.

Students will be graded on based on their technical and practical knowledge, and they are expected to learn and develop skills and behavior fit for a scientist who is logical, analytical, and ethical with compassion for the world around them. They will also be expected to simultaneously be both curious and respectful of the world around them and to avoid destroying their environment to satiate their curiosity.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
