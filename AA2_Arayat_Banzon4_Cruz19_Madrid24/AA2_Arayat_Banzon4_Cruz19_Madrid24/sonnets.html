Metathesiophobia: A fear of change.

Winds of Change

Trembling in fear, chill sets in my bones
A fleeting sentiment akin to my sorrow
For as I direct my gaze upon the unknown
I see hope hovering above my tomorrow
Rain or shine, night or day
I find myself no longer frightened
So go forth and melodiously hum away!
And I too shall partake in this act of godsend
In my heart and mind, I dream of a place
Where gaiety and merriment reign
Plunged into darkness, we again awake
To ascend and live with no shame
Allow the world to brighten once more
With happiness, cheer, and fun galore!

Made by Gianna Cruz.
Meadow of Memory

In the meadow of all those who rest,
A memory waits, rusted away with age
Now merely an ache inside a chest,
For nothing can escape from the cage of change.
Oh, how pitiful is the sea’s yearning
Nightly does it raise its tides higher
Driven mad by the moon’s calling
As the sun ties us to the pyre
Life’s changing ever so slightly
Nature’s burning down
Earth’s spinning nightly
Even as it starts to drown
In the meadow shall our memories forever dance offbeat
Changing each day, no matter how bittersweet.

Made by Azillana Madrid.

These Stagnant Years

As I traverse this land in my mortal body
I yearn to profess my compassion and goodwill!
And ye of ethereal splendor, with such divine skill!
I offer these gifts unto thee, o my fair lady
I long to touch the sky and forever bask in its beauty
To savor life’s blessings, with allies traitorous to a nil
New beginnings on the horizon, my fantasies they fill
Second chances washed away, such a cruel reality!
And yes, you must be sorely mistaken!
For I sit alone, in everlasting solitude
Faced with the consequences of my evils
I dream of a future in simple residence
At my behest, new comrades of multitude
Eyes on the sunrise, in silence so peaceful

Made by Gianna Cruz.
The Next Page

The fate of man has been obscured
Yet the dread of the end grows ever so closer
Oh, I have never been one to get closure
My happily ever after not once ensured
Any path I take will have no return assured
Nothing to make what is ahead any clearer
I’m filled with hesitation to take any step further
Indecision causing all the pain I’ve endured
But what worth shall stasis bring me?
Continue on with confusion!
For life shall always be chaotic and free
For the past is as constant as the sea
No one reads a book from its conclusion
So let the story continue with glee.

Made by Azillana Madrid.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
