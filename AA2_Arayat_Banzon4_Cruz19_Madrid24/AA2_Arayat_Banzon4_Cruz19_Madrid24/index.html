Christmas Club: Limited Edition

Nov 19, 2024

It's the merry season, and the Christmas Club is coming back to town! This club will only have sessions from November 24 - Dec 20, and if you miss out on all the fun activities, you'll have to wait until next year to join! This extra-curricular is meant to boost student morale and spirit during times of stress and burn out.

Work Smarter, Not Harder

Oct 11, 2024

Exam Week will slowly be upon us, so we've taken the the time to remind both students and staff to not overwork themselves and lose sleep over something like grades. While academic achievement is important, it is secondary to your health and safety, and this includes your mental health too.

Another reminder is to respect your teachers' time aswell and submit your assessments on time. This also means that you should avoid contacting your teachers and supervisors outside of working hours, because they too have a life. Respect is a two-way street, so if you want to avoid teachers giving you homework for the weekend, then don't bother them on their off days.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
