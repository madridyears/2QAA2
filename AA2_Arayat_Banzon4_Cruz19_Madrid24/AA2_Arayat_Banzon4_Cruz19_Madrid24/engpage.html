English 3


What does English 3 provide the students?

Each student enrolled in English 3 will have the opportunity to acquire the skills required to effectively communicate in English and express their thoughts, feelings, and desires. Every student at River Academy is given the tools necessary to develop their ability to speak and convey their ideas as well as their experiences.

At River Academy, students have more than enough resources to enhance their critical thinking skills, reading comprehension, and ability to closely examine papers for signs of plagiarism.

How does English 3 test the knowledge of their students?

English 3 makes use of standardized tests, practical assessments, and summative examinations to test the knowledge of their students and to ensure that they have acquired the necessary fundamental skills and knowledge they need. However, we do take into factor the physical and mental health of our students to create an educational environment that is both sympathetic and fair to the student so they may grow into a well developed and stable member of society.

Students will be graded on based on their profiency and creativity, and they are expected to learn and develop skills and behavior fit for a writer who is creative, imaginative, and passionate.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
