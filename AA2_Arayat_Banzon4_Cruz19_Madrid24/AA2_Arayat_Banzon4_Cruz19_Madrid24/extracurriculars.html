Curriculum


Peer Facilitators

In order to provide students with the chance to help their friends in need of knowledge, socialisation, and psychological "first aid" through peer counselling sessions, the school established this club.

Booklovers

This club was established by the school to encourage students to use the library and other resources wisely and to share their love of literary analysis and other related activities.

Curtain Call

This club was established by the school to provide students with additional opportunities to study and develop in the performing arts. Students who want to learn more about the theatre, performing arts, and related topics can join this group.

Mathsketeers

The purpose of this club is to assist students in connecting with mathematics by allowing them to work with fellow mathematicians, share their ideas, and explore mathematical concepts.

Test Tubes

The school started this group to encourage students' interest in biology and provide them with opportunity to acquire the skills required to become scientists.

Badminton Club

This club was created by the school for students to learn badminton etiquette and be able to display sportmanship no matter the outcome.

About Us

Established in 1987, we are one of the top medical sciences based academies in Quezon City. Our founder Dr. George Tolentino I wanted to create a hub for advancing medical knowledge and fostering collaboration among healthcare professionals. With a commitment to excellence, we continue to empower the next generation of medical practitioners through innovative education and research initiatives. We aspire to raise empathetic, compassionate, capable, and skilled professionals who will lead the medical field to a brighter future.
